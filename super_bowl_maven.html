Maven Analytics Super bowl challenge

For this challenge, we had to analyze the 249 Super Bowl commercials from the year 2000 to 2021 and recommend a data-driven strategy for the Maven Motors Super Bowl spot.

Here are some key insights from the analysis:
      * Bud Light brand had the maximum number of commercials during the Super Bowl
      * Bud Light was the brand which showcased the products quickly through their ads
      * Other than NFL, any commercial which had the highest Youtube views were having an average duration less than 60 secs
      * Most of the commercials with highest Youtube views had funny content
      * The car brand commercials had lesser views compared to the other brands
      * Car commercials has better views on TV when compared to Youtube
      * Doritos was on top of the list in terms of Youtube views and likes
      * Using animals and fun content will have more views for the commercials
      * The Maven Motors should consider having their commercial shorter and focus on fun element to grab more attention from viewers
