Space challenge

The space dataset contained details of all the space missions that took place between 1957 until 2022. The dataset included information about countries, companies who launched them, budget for each mission etc.,

The analysis process involved a lot of data cleaning as there were many spacial characters in the mission names and the country names and then DAX formulas were used in order to calculate the success and failure rate for each mission.
