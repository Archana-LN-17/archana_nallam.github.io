Formula 1 racing analysis Onyx Data

This challenge was introduced by Onyx Data DNA and Astrato analtyics on LinkeIn platform. The purpose of the challenge was to analyze the Formula 1 (F1) racing results dataset. This was an definitely an interesting dataset to work on as I had to spend more time on understanding various terms used in the F1 racing, in order to be able to come up with some useful insights.

Data cleaning was performed after this to present the details in a better way.
