Books Analysis challenge D'Challenger

The analysis is done on the Books published during the past century. The dataset includes information like the books titles, authors, Year of Publications, Publishers names etc.,

Some key insights from the analysis:
      * The dataset had about 11,127 books details from the year 1900 to 2020
      * Year 2006 had the maximum number of books published after which the number declined again
      *Vintage publishers had published the maximum number of books
      *The book with highest rating and reviews was "Twilight" by Stephen Meyer
      * P. G. Wodehouse and Stephen King were the authors with highest number of books
